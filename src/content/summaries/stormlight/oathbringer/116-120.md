### 116: Alone
Kaladin lands on the marble bridge in Thaylen City alone. He draws in Stormlight and engages six Fused, ancient singers who can fly, in aerial combat. Dalinar Kholin walks through the Low Ward as a second thunderclast, a thirty-foot stone monster, breaks through the city wall. A falling boulder pins Dalinar's arm, but he uses Stormlight to heal and escape. The thunderclast attacks the Gemstone Reserve and scatters Thaylenah’s wealth across the city.

Fused sliding on the ground like they are on grease steal the King’s Drop ruby from a palanquin. Dalinar realizes the soldiers outside with glowing red eyes are actually Sadeas's army. Jasnah Kholin discovers Renarin bonding a corrupted spren of Odium in the temple of Pailiah. Shallan Davar creates twenty illusions to distract the Fused on the bridge while Adolin and the spren Pattern follow. Shallan uses a gemstone bead to cross the sea of souls toward the Oathgate platform.

Venli watches the second thunderclast move toward the city walls while Odium stands beside her as a force of golden light. Kaladin disarms a Fused and takes its long spear to continue the aerial fight near obsidian mountains. In the Makabak fleet, Emperor Yanagawn argues with his advisors until he learns the Alethi have apparently betrayed the Thaylens. Shallan reaches the Oathgate but the two building-sized spren gatekeepers refuse her passage because the portal is locked. Szeth-son-Neturo hovers above the battlefield with his master Nin and discusses the parshmen's legal right to the land.



### 117: Champion with Nine Shadows
Dalinar walks onto the rubble of the broken city wall to face the Sadeas army alone. The Reshi Radiant Lift joins him, carrying a Shardblade that appears as a silver pole. Dalinar tasks Lift with stealing back the King's Drop ruby from the sliding Fused. Odium approaches Dalinar with a group of Fused and a smoky shadow spren. Adolin fights a Fused on the bridge but is stabbed through the stomach with a lance.

Adolin's deadeye spren, a scratching woman with vines for skin, attacks the Fused to save him. Shallan summons a stone wall by manifesting the soul of a bead from her pocket. The Fused woman with the ruby mocks Lift's lack of grace while sliding across the rock. Nin tells Szeth he must choose an external code or person to follow. Szeth decides to follow Dalinar Kholin instead of the law and engages the enemy army.

Kaladin runs out of Stormlight and falls into the sea of beads but is rescued by Syl and Shallan. Navani Kholin watches from the wall as Highprince Amaram leads his army of turncoats toward the city. Dalinar demands a contest of champions from Odium to decide the fate of Roshar. Lift is crushed by a thunderclast's strike while trying to retrieve the ruby. Szeth arrives and destroys the thunderclast with his black Shardblade, Nightblood, which leaks black smoke.



### 118: The Weight of it All
Odium touches Dalinar's shoulder and forces him to relive his most violent memories through the Thrill. Dalinar sees himself murdering soldiers and innocent people during the conquest of Alethkar. Odium reveals he influenced Dalinar's actions and offers to take away his emotional pain. Dalinar watches the vision of the Rift burning and hears his wife Evi's death screams. A bolt of lightning strikes Dalinar's copy of The Way of Kings and shreds the book.

Szeth uses Nightblood to vaporize enemies but the sword begins to drain his Stormlight and turn his arm black. Navani uses a painrial fabrial to incapacitate soldiers who have captured Queen Fen. Ash, the Herald Shalash, finds the Herald Taln repeating his Desolation warning in an Alethi tent. Renarin sees a vision of Jasnah killing him in the temple and waits for her strike. Odium announces he has chosen Dalinar to be his champion with nine shadows.

Dalinar refuses to give his pain to Odium and insists on taking responsibility for his actions. Jasnah chooses not to kill Renarin and embraces him instead. Renarin realizes his visions of the future can be wrong because Jasnah spared him. Dalinar finds a single golden gloryspren in his hand and prepares to take the next step. He shouts that Odium cannot have his pain, defying the god's attempt to claim him.



### 119: Unity
Dalinar accepts the guilt of burning Rathalas and killing Evi as part of his personal growth. He reaches into the Cognitive and Spiritual Realms and slams them together. This action creates Honor's Perpendicularity, a pillar of golden light that recharges every gemstone in the city. The three realms merging creates a highway of glass across the sea of beads for the trapped Radiants. Odium screams in frustration as he realizes Dalinar has become Unity.

Teft swears the Third Ideal of the Windrunners in the darkness of Urithiru and arrives through the Oathgate with reinforcements. Ash explains to Taln that four thousand years have passed since the Heralds abandoned him. Taln views the long peace as a gift to humanity rather than a betrayal. Lift breathes Stormlight into Szeth to save him from Nightblood's hunger. Jasnah Soulcasts attacking soldiers into crystal and smoke while moving through the city.

Seven Radiants and two Heralds gather around Dalinar at the broken city wall. Amaram swallows a smokestone containing the Unmade Yelig-nar to gain power. Dalinar orders the Radiants to defend the city and open the Oathgate for more troops. Shallan creates a massive illusionary army to distract the remaining Sadeas soldiers. Dalinar walks toward the red mist of the Thrill to confront the Unmade Nerga-oul.



### 120: The Spear that would not Break
Kaladin engages Amaram, who now has violet crystals growing through his Shardplate and skin. Adolin helps the Thaylen Shardbearer Hrdalm fight the remaining thunderclast in the city streets. Jasnah Soulcasts a breach in the wall into a solid section of polished bronze. Szeth and Lift use a fake ruby to trick the Fused and secure the King's Drop. Renarin kills the thunderclast by blinding it with a beacon of Stormlight.

Shallan merges her identities with Veil and Radiant to maintain the illusionary army until she is exhausted. Amaram's Shardplate explodes as he transforms into a monster with a gemstone heart. Kaladin stabs the gemstone in Amaram's chest with a spear, causing the highlord to fall. Rock arrives with a Shardbow and shoots Amaram through the head and heart to finish him. Dalinar captures the Thrill inside the King’s Drop ruby, causing the red mist to vanish.

The Sadeas soldiers lose their bloodlust and many collapse as the Unmade is withdrawn. The Everstorm thins and passes over the island without its previous destructive force. Fused and parshman troops retreat toward their ships as the battle ends. Dalinar weeps with joy while cradling the glowing ruby on the field of stone. The Radiants reunite as sunlight finally breaks through the clouds over Thaylen City.
