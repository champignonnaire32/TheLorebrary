### Chapter 61
Riders and dragons gather at the Basgiath ridgeline as a massive wyvern horde approaches at dawn. Violet stands with Fourth Wing while her mother, General Lilith Sorrengail, prepares the defense at the gates. The venin-led horde is larger than expected, prompting the deployment of infantry throughout the college courtyards. Violet carries twelve daggers and a handheld crossbow, coordinating with Xaden and Rhiannon to defend their assigned sector. Lilith uses her signet to create a storm, providing Violet with an atmospheric source for her lightning power.

Violet releases a massive lightning strike into the upper cloud cover, killing several wyvern instantly. Tairn and Sgaeyl launch from the ridge to engage the enemy in close-quarters aerial combat. Tairn bites the throat of a wyvern while Violet kills its venin rider with a precise lightning strike. Sawyer and Sliseag are swarmed by three wyvern, one of which carries a dark wielder. Violet continues to wield lightning strikes for surrounding sectors, hitting two-thirds of her intended targets.

Three venin on an assassination mission fly directly toward Violet and Xaden’s position. Rhiannon and the squad work in synchronized pairs to distribute weapons and defend each other. Tairn engages a wyvern head-on, allowing a venin to jump from the enemy creature onto Tairn’s neck. Violet attempts to unbuckle her saddle strap to fight the intruder, but the rain-swollen leather becomes stuck. Rhiannon leaps from her own dragon, Feirge, onto Tairn’s back to intercept the venin before he reaches Violet.



### Chapter 62
Rhiannon engages the venin on Tairn’s back while the dragon continues a rapid descent. Violet hands Rhiannon a dagger and then uses her handheld crossbow to shoot the venin in the chest. The venin falls to his death, and Rhiannon returns to her dragon after retrieving her weapon. General Melgren arrives with reinforcements as the battle over Basgiath becomes a chaotic melee. A wyvern bites off Sawyer’s leg below the knee, causing him to lose his grip and begin falling.

Violet cuts herself free from her saddle and leaps from Tairn onto the attacking wyvern’s neck. She jumps again onto Sliseag to catch the unconscious Sawyer as they both tumble into free fall. Tairn dives and catches Violet and Sawyer in his claw, carrying them safely to the ground. Violet applies a tourniquet to Sawyer’s stump while gryphon fliers arrive to transport the wounded. Cat and Maren take Sawyer to the healers while Violet encounters a venin asim named Wynn.

Cat uses her signet to amplify Wynn’s greed, forcing the venin to talk about her orders to capture Violet. A more powerful venin appears, kills Wynn for her failure, and uses magic to immobilize Violet in midair. The dark wielder claims that the venin leader, the Sage, has specific plans for Violet’s power. Andarna emerges from the cliffside, revealing she has matured into a large dragon with black scales. She lunges toward the venin to protect Violet as the first wave of the wyvern horde recedes.



### Chapter 63
Andarna breathes fire and decapitates the venin attacker before he can capture the immobilized Violet. Violet notices that Andarna’s scales are shifting color and that she has matured into a full-sized dragon. Xaden and Violet discuss the battle, noting that the largest wave of wyvern is still waiting to attack. Jesinia Neilwart reveals a discrepancy in the journals stating that seven dragon breeds are required to raise wards. Violet realizes Andarna is the missing seventh breed and must contribute her fire to the Basgiath wardstone.

Violet and Xaden argue with General Melgren and Lilith Sorrengail about defending the wardstone. Melgren plans to abandon Basgiath to defend the Vale, but Violet insists the wards can be raised. Lilith agrees to let Violet attempt to activate the wardstone if Brennan can mend the physical object. Jesinia is sent to the healers to watch over the wounded Sawyer while the fliers prepare for a ground assault. Xaden and Tairn stay at the gates to confront the incoming venin leader and the final horde.

Violet runs to the wardstone chamber and finds that Brennan has successfully mended the shattered iron stone. Andarna reveals she waited centuries to hatch specifically for Violet and descends into the ward chamber. Violet discovers that the stone can hold power but requires massive imbuing that would take weeks. Tairn shares his visual perspective with Violet, showing her that Xaden is losing ground against the Sage. Violet begins channeling Tairn’s power into the stone, ignoring the physical toll on her own body.



### Chapter 64
Xaden faces the Sage at the ravine's edge while the wyvern horde prepares for a final strike. Violet discovers that the wardstone requires the fire of six dragon breeds plus one additional breed. Andarna breathes her golden fire into the wardstone, fulfilling her role as the seventh breed. Violet channels Tairn’s power into the stone so aggressively that she begins to experience magical burnout. Brennan attempts to mend Violet’s failing body as she continues to push power into the iron structure.

General Lilith Sorrengail arrives at the ward chamber and realizes Violet is dying from the imbuing process. Lilith kicks Violet away from the stone to break the connection and save her daughter’s life. Sloane Mairi is ordered by Lilith to use her siphon signet to transfer power from a living source. Lilith commands Sloane to drain the life and power from her and her dragon, Aimsir, into the stone. Sloane siphons Lilith’s entire life force, causing the general to collapse and die as the stone activates.

The Basgiath wards rise, creating a black flame on the stone and a magical barrier over the college. Every wyvern on the battlefield falls dead as the magical connection to their creators is severed. The remaining venin riders retreat as they lose access to their power within the warded area. Mira and Brennan mourn over their mother’s body as the first-years guard the ward chamber. Violet collapses from exhaustion, sensing that Xaden has survived the duel with the Sage.



### Chapter 65
Wyvern carcasses fall onto Basgiath as the college celebrates the successful activation of the wards. Brennan drapes his flight jacket over Lilith’s body while Mira sobs over their mother’s death. Tairn assures Violet that Xaden is alive and waiting for her near the edge of the ravine. Rhiannon and the rest of the squad reunite, reporting that Sawyer is stable despite his injury. Leadership begins negotiating the terms of the riders' return to Basgiath now that the threat is neutralized.

Violet finds Xaden standing alone at the ravine’s edge where he killed the venin leader. Xaden reveals that he snapped the Sage’s neck and watched the body fall into the river below. He expresses remorse for Lilith’s death despite her history of executing the parents of the marked ones. Xaden explains that it is safer for Violet at Basgiath now that the wards are fully functional. Violet notices that Xaden is acting distant and avoids making direct eye contact with her.

Xaden finally looks at Violet, revealing a faint red ring around his gold-flecked onyx irises. Violet realizes that Xaden turned into a venin during his desperate struggle to defeat the Sage. Xaden tells Violet she should be scared of him because of the change in his nature. A missive from Nolon Colbersy to Lilith confirms there is no known cure for those who turn. The war for the Continent continues as the primary characters face the reality of Xaden’s transformation.
