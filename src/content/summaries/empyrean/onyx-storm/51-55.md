### 51: Chapter 1
Xaden and Violet wake to an immediate wyvern attack on Riorson House. Brennan coordinates the defense while Xaden assumes command as the Duke of Tyrrendor. Violet is assigned to the north gate with her squad to protect the city walls. Aaric pulls Violet aside and insists she must protect the temple of Dunne outside the walls to save the province. Tairn launches into the dark, rain-filled sky to intercept the approaching greenfire wyvern.

Violet uses her glass conduit to channel lightning, illuminating the battlefield for her fellow riders. She identifies four pairs of wyvern testing the strength of Aretia's wards. Tairn executes a high-speed roll to snap the neck of a wyvern during an aerial engagement. Violet spots a group of wyvern flying low toward the field where Andarna is waiting. She forces Tairn into a steep dive to reach the temple before the enemy.

Violet channels a massive surge of power and splits a lightning bolt to incinerate two wyvern. She observes that one of the remaining wyvern is carrying a silver-haired Maven, a high-ranking venin commander. Tairn slams his weight onto a target as the sky fills with a flurry of wings. A piercing screech echoes through the air as Tairn bellows in sudden pain. The impact throws Violet forward in her saddle as the battle reaches the temple steps.





### 52: Chapter 2
Tairn is grounded after a wyvern’s wingspur, a barbed tail spike, embeds in his thigh. Rhiannon and Feirge attempt to chase the Maven, Theophanie, but Violet orders them to stay back. Andarna reveals she disobeyed orders to guard the temple based on Aaric's earlier suggestion. Rhiannon utilizes her signet to telepathically pull the massive wingspur out of Tairn’s leg. Tairn remains unable to launch as more wyvern approach the defenseless temple.

Theophanie, a venin Maven with silver hair, appears at the temple steps and offers Violet a truce. She reveals her past as a priestess of Dunne and claims she can give Violet what she wants most. Two venin in red robes join Theophanie, but Andarna’s fire fails to damage them. A sudden ripple of magic signals the activation of Aretia's wards, causing the wyvern to fall. Theophanie flees in terror when she realizes the dragons have successfully fired the wardstone.

A wyvern carcass crashes into the temple and destroys the central pillar, sending debris everywhere. Xaden arrives and uses his shadow signet to suspend the falling marble in mid-air. Leothan, a shimmering irid dragon from the rare seventh breed with shifting scales, manifests from the shadows. He admits to firing the wardstone to protect Andarna, whom he considers family. Xaden reunites with Violet and confirms that the city is finally protected by the new wards.





### 53: Chapter 3
Leothan restricts his conversation to Andarna and Violet, excluding the other riders and dragons. He explains that he is of the same bloodline as Andarna and has been observing her from afar. Leothan offers to take Andarna across the sea to her ancestral home to learn the ways of the irid. He insists that Andarna must break her bond with Violet to leave with him. The irid claims his kind has the power to bend and sever magical ties at will.

Leothan reveals that Tairn bonded Violet first, making Andarna’s second connection a unique irid feat. Violet realizes Andarna yearns for the acceptance of her own kind and encourages her to go. She promises Andarna that she wants her to be happy and safe even if they are apart. Leothan uses his magic to cut the mental link between the two, leaving Violet in silence. Andarna and Leothan launch into the sky and vanish, blending their scales into the clouds.

Violet collapses as the loss of her second dragon bond leaves her hollow and adrift. Tairn forces his strength down the remaining bond to prevent Violet from dying or catatonia. Xaden catches Violet and grounds her with his own emotions to anchor her heartbeat. Rhiannon watches in horror as her friend is shattered by the sudden separation. The chapter concludes with the stark reality that Andarna is gone and the bond is severed.





### 54: Chapter 4
Violet remains bedbound in Riorson House for three days, paralyzed by the loss of Andarna. Brennan and the squad take turns watching over her while the rest of Aretia celebrates the wards. Xaden holds Violet every night, using his presence to keep her anchored to reality. Mira arrives on the fourth day and forces Violet to bathe and face the world. Violet learns that Xaden has committed treason by opening Tyrrendor’s borders to thousands of refugees.

The western front is crumbling, and wyvern are pushing toward the city of Draithus. Mira and Brennan argue about their parents’ secret attempt to dedicate Violet to Dunne. It is revealed that their father took infant Violet to a temple in Unnbriel for protection. A prophecy from that event claimed a heart beating "for or within" Violet would turn dark. Mira accuses Violet of being at risk, but Xaden interrupts to reveal his own secret.

Xaden confesses that he turned venin, a soul-draining dark wielder, at Basgiath to save Violet's life. Mira draws her alloy-hilted dagger and threatens to kill Xaden for his transformation. Violet steps between them, insisting that Xaden is stable and still in control of himself. Mira leaves Aretia in a rage, warning Violet that Xaden will eventually kill her. Violet realizes her father knew about the threat of venin and tried to protect her through the temple.





### 55: Chapter 5
Xaden cuts off Talladium, a rare metal required for forging dragon-slaying weapons, to force an alliance with King Tauri. Violet returns to the valley to resume her studies with the squad and Tairn. She struggles to practice runes because Tairn’s immense power is too unruly for delicate work. Quinn suggests that Violet should use a more aggressive and brutal approach to temper her runes. Violet successfully creates a hardening rune by forcing the magic into shape with raw power.

Felix takes Violet to the field to practice her signet and criticizes her combat mistakes. He reveals that her tendency to chase civilians nearly caused a disaster at the city walls. Violet learns to split a lightning bolt into two accurate branches during a target exercise. Bodhi argues with Xaden about dropping out of school to fight on the front lines. Xaden refuses and demands that Bodhi graduate so he can eventually succeed him as Duke.

Violet realizes Xaden is training a replacement because he does not expect to survive long-term. Melgren sends a missive warning that entering the upcoming battle will result in total loss. The high priestess of Dunne’s temple writes to Xaden to offer him a favor of his choosing. King Tauri demands that Tyrrendor restore the flow of Talladium or face dire consequences. Xaden and Violet share a moment of intimacy in the assembly hall before facing the looming war.
