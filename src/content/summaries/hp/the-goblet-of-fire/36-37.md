### 36: The Parting of the Ways
Dumbledore binds Barty Crouch with magical ropes and instructs Professor McGonagall to stand guard over him. He sends Snape to fetch Madam Pomfrey and Minister Cornelius Fudge for questioning. Harry accompanies Dumbledore to the headmaster's office where Sirius Black waits in his human form. Fawkes the phoenix provides comfort to Harry while Dumbledore insists Harry recount the night's events immediately. Harry describes the graveyard ritual, Voldemort’s rebirth, and the appearance of the Death Eaters.

Dumbledore explains the Reverse Spell effect, Priori Incantatem, which occurred because Harry and Voldemort’s wands share phoenix feather cores. The connection forced Voldemort's wand to produce echoes of its recent victims, including Cedric, Bertha Jorkins, and Harry’s parents. Fawkes heals Harry’s leg wound with tears before Dumbledore takes Harry and Sirius to the hospital wing. Harry receives a dreamless sleep potion from Madam Pomfrey and falls into a heavy slumber. Cornelius Fudge later arrives at the hospital wing accompanied by a Dementor that immediately sucks out Barty Crouch’s soul.

Fudge refuses to believe Voldemort has returned and dismisses Harry’s testimony as the hallucinations of a disturbed boy. Dumbledore warns Fudge that the Dementors will join Voldemort and urges him to remove them from Azkaban and seek alliance with giants. Snape reveals the Dark Mark on his own arm to Fudge as proof of the Dark Lord's return. Fudge leaves the school after giving Harry the thousand-galleon tournament prize money in a bag. Dumbledore sends Sirius to alert the "old crowd" and gives Snape a secret, dangerous mission.

### 37: The Beginning
Harry meets with the Diggorys the next morning and offers them the tournament prize money, but they refuse it. He returns to Gryffindor Tower and observes that most students are avoiding him due to Rita Skeeter’s articles. Ron informs Harry that Dumbledore insists Harry return to the Dursleys for the start of the summer. Harry visits Hagrid, who reveals he has a secret mission involving Madame Maxime and the giants. At the Leaving Feast, the Great Hall is draped in black in memory of Cedric Diggory.

Dumbledore addresses the school, publicly identifies Voldemort as Cedric’s murderer, and honors Harry’s bravery in returning the body. He warns the students that they must remain united against the discord Voldemort will spread. On the Hogwarts Express, Hermione reveals she caught Rita Skeeter, who is an unregistered Animagus capable of turning into a beetle. Hermione keeps the beetle in a sealed glass jar protected by an Unbreakable Charm to prevent Rita from writing for a year. Malfoy, Crabbe, and Goyle enter the compartment to taunt Harry about the Dark Lord's return.

Harry, Ron, and Hermione simultaneously hex the trio, leaving them unconscious and covered in tentacles and boils. Fred and George join the compartment and reveal that Ludo Bagman cheated them using vanishing leprechaun gold. Bagman has fled to escape goblin creditors after losing a bet that Harry would win the tournament outright. Harry forces the twins to take his thousand-galleon prize money to fund their future joke shop. At King's Cross, Harry says goodbye to his friends and Mrs. Weasley before departing with Uncle Vernon.
