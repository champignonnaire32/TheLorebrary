### 31: O.W.L.s
Ron celebrates the Quidditch win while Harry and Hermione worry about Grawp. June arrives and the fifth-year students begin their O.W.L. examinations. The examiners arrive at Hogwarts, including Griselda Marchbanks and Professor Tofty. Harry takes his Charms and Transfiguration theory and practical exams. He performs a Patronus for a bonus point in Defense Against the Dark Arts.

Hermione frets over her Ancient Runes exam while Hagrid continues teaching classes. During the Astronomy practical at night, Umbridge leads a group of wizards to Hagrid's cabin. They attempt to stun Hagrid, but the spells bounce off his giant-blood skin. Professor McGonagall runs to intervene but is hit by four stunning spells to the chest. Hagrid escapes into the darkness with Fang draped around his shoulders.

The students discuss the attack and McGonagall's transfer to the hospital wing. Harry sits for his final exam, History of Magic, in the hot Great Hall. He falls asleep and enters a vision of the Department of Mysteries. He sees Voldemort torturing Sirius in the room full of glass spheres. Harry wakes up screaming and falls off his desk, ending the examination.



### 32: Out of the Fire
Harry insists to Professor Tofty that he is fine and rushes to the hospital wing. Madam Pomfrey informs him that Professor McGonagall has been transferred to St. Mungo's hospital. Harry finds Ron and Hermione and describes his vision of Sirius being tortured at the Ministry. Hermione argues that the vision might be a trap designed by Voldemort. Ginny and Luna join the group and offer to help verify Sirius's location.

The group decides to use the Floo Network in Umbridge's office to contact Grimmauld Place. Ron distracts Umbridge by claiming Peeves is destroying the Transfiguration department. Ginny and Luna act as lookouts in the corridor, claiming there is garrotting gas present. Harry and Hermione sneak into the office using the Invisibility Cloak. Harry sticks his head into the fire and speaks to Kreacher at number twelve.

Kreacher gleefully tells Harry that Sirius has gone to the Department of Mysteries. Umbridge suddenly drags Harry out of the fire by his hair and captures the group. The Inquisitorial Squad holds Ron, Ginny, Neville, and Luna captive in the office. Umbridge summons Snape and demands Veritaserum to force the truth from Harry. Snape claims he has no more potion and ignores Harry's coded message about Padfoot.



### 33: Fight and Flight
Umbridge threatens Harry with the Cruciatus Curse to reveal who he was contacting. Hermione fakes a crying fit and claims they were building a weapon for Dumbledore. She leads Umbridge into the Forbidden Forest while Harry follows closely behind. Hermione takes them deep into the woods toward the area inhabited by the centaurs. A herd of fifty centaurs surrounds them and demands to know their business.

Umbridge insults the centaurs by calling them "filthy half-breeds" and "beasts" of "near-human intelligence." She uses a binding spell on Magorian, inciting a violent retaliatory attack from the herd. The centaurs destroy Umbridge's wand and carry her screaming into the depths of the forest. The herd then turns on Harry and Hermione, accusing them of using the centaurs as servants. Grawp the giant wanders into the clearing and disrupts the confrontation by calling for Hagrid.

Grawp's presence allows Harry and Hermione to escape while the centaurs fire arrows at the giant. They reunite with Ron, Ginny, Neville, and Luna, who escaped the Inquisitorial Squad using hexes. Luna suggests they fly to the Ministry of Magic on the backs of invisible Thestrals. The group locates several Thestrals attracted by the scent of blood on Harry's clothes. Harry, Ron, Hermione, Ginny, Neville, and Luna mount the skeletal horses and take flight toward London.



### 34: The Department of Mysteries
The six students fly across the country on Thestrals and land near a red telephone box. They enter the box and dial the code to access the Ministry of Magic visitors' entrance. The Atrium is completely deserted as they exit the elevator and head for the golden gates. They take another lift down to the ninth floor and enter the Department of Mysteries. The group finds themselves in a circular black room with twelve handleless doors.

The walls of the room rotate whenever a door is closed, making navigation difficult. They first enter a room containing a large tank filled with floating, sentient brains. After retreating, they enter a room with a sunken stone pit and a tattered black veil. Harry and Luna hear whispering voices coming from behind the veil, but Hermione forces them away. They find a locked door that melts Harry's knife before entering a room filled with clocks.

They pass through the clock room and finally enter the massive Hall of Prophecy. Harry leads the group to row ninety-seven, where he expected to find Sirius being tortured. The aisle is empty, but Ron discovers a glass sphere with Harry's name on it. Harry retrieves the prophecy, which contains a record of a message from Trelawney to Dumbledore. A dozen masked Death Eaters emerge from the darkness and surround the six students.



### 35: Beyond the Veil
Lucius Malfoy demands that Harry hand over the prophecy while Bellatrix Lestrange mocks him. The Death Eaters reveal that the vision of Sirius was a trap to lure Harry to the Ministry. Harry refuses to surrender the orb and orders his friends to smash the surrounding shelves. The glass spheres shatter, releasing ghostly figures of Seers that fill the air with echoing voices. The students sprint through the collapsing shelves and attempt to reach the exit doors.

The group becomes separated as they battle Death Eaters across various rooms of the department. Hermione is knocked unconscious by a purple flame spell cast by Antonin Dolohov. Neville's nose is broken, and Ron is attacked by a brain that wraps its tentacles around him. Ginny suffers a broken ankle, and Luna is stunned while attempting to seal a door. Harry and Neville end up in the Death Chamber, where the remaining Death Eaters corner them.

Members of the Order of the Phoenix arrive and engage the Death Eaters in a fierce duel. Neville accidentally smashes the prophecy during the struggle, and the message is lost in the noise. Sirius Black fights Bellatrix Lestrange on the stone dais in the center of the room. Bellatrix hits Sirius with a spell that sends him backward through the tattered black veil. Sirius disappears behind the curtain and fails to reappear on the other side.
