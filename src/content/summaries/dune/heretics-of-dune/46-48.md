### 46: Theodore Roethke
Odrade and Sheeana escape the Honoured Matre attack on the penthouse by descending a deep airshaft using clusters of glowglobes for lift. They emerge into a pump room and seek a passage to the desert through the building's interconnected machinery systems. Odrade confirms the death of the Mother Superior and declares herself the temporary successor to the position. Meanwhile, Miles Teg enters a state of hyper-accelerated movement and mental awareness to combat the Honoured Matre threat. He slaughters over fifty people in the headquarters building and seizes the control room for the local defenses.

Teg escapes to an alley five kilometers away where he notices his blood has turned black from extreme oxygenation. He enters a restaurant called "Personal Service" where he is recognized by a waiter who served under him at Renditai. The waiter treats Teg’s injured hand and provides him with a massive amount of food to sustain his metabolism. Teg observes Professor Delnay dining nearby and identifies him as a scholar of martial arts and history. He invites the professor to join him and asks for help reaching a rendezvous point for his former soldiers.

Delnay disguises Teg as a sewage-covered Bordano overseer and leads him through a storm to a crowded bar. Teg cleans himself and dons his full military regalia before entering the main room to meet his gathered veterans. He informs the soldiers that they are going to capture a no-ship belonging to the people of the Scattering. Teg uses his new doubled vision to perceive the exact locations of every no-ship on and around the planet Gammu. He assigns a small group to guard the bar and leads the rest of the armed veterans to execute the assault.





### 47: Leto I: Bene Gesserit Archives
Teg lands a captured no-ship on the sands of Rakis near a rock formation formerly known as Wind Trap. He deploys armored ornithopters and ground cars while his officers express doubt about his plan to meet Odrade in the desert. Teg maintains his command through his new ability to see the movements of his enemies and allies without physical instruments. He refuses to see Burzmali immediately but permits Lucilla to enter the command station for an explanation. Lucilla is visibly angry and demands to know why they have diverted to this remote location instead of a main center.

Teg informs Lucilla that Mother Superior Taraza has been killed and that the Honoured Matres now control the Rakian cities. He explains that he is at this specific site to pick up Odrade, Sheeana, and a captive sandworm. Odrade and Sheeana arrive riding on the back of a worm which they steer into the no-ship’s massive hold. Teg tells Lucilla that she must take the ship and the ghola to a safe location while he stays behind. He plans to create a massive military diversion that will provoke the Honoured Matres into sterilizing the entire planet.

Teg orders Burzmali to take command of the no-ship and gives him instructions to follow Odrade’s lead. He says a brief farewell to Odrade and kisses her cheek while revealing that he knows Taraza’s full plan for the planet. Odrade realizes that Teg intends to die on Rakis to ensure the Sisterhood’s escape with the last remaining worm. The no-ship lifts off as Teg prepares to lead his ground forces in a final suicidal resistance against the whores. This action effectively turns Rakis into a charred ruin, wiping out all humans, worms, and sandtrout left on the surface.





### 48: Mother Superior Taraza, Argument
Odrade sits in the late Taraza’s office at the Chapter House and integrates the shared memories of her predecessor. She reflects on how Taraza leaked information to the Honoured Matres to lure them into the destruction of Rakis. The Sisterhood has successfully transitioned to a fortress position to survive the impending conflict with the forces from the Scattering. Odrade observes the bust of Chenoeh in her office and considers the historical weight of her new role as Mother Superior. She acknowledges the death of Miles Teg and the Tleilaxu Master Waff during the final transition on the desert planet.

The Bene Gesserit Council discusses the "proof of Siona" found in Sheeana’s cells which grants her immunity from prescient detection. Duncan Idaho remains confined within the grounded no-ship because he lacks this specific genetic protection and could be tracked. Odrade plans to transform the Chapter House planet into a new desert habitat to support the captive worm’s lifecycle. Sheeana will lure the worm into a basin of melange to trigger its metamorphosis into sandtrout. Odrade prepares a new bust of Miles Teg to honor him as the "Great Heretic" for his role in their survival.

Odrade visits Duncan Idaho in the no-ship and finds him listless and mourning the death of the Bashar. She explains that the destruction of Rakis was necessary to eliminate the oracular influence of the Tyrant’s remaining awareness. Duncan refuses to serve as a breeding partner for the Sisterhood and mocks Odrade’s claim that she can feel love. Odrade informs him that Murbella is pregnant and will be trained as a Reverend Mother due to her genetic potential. She then travels to the ship's hold to observe the captive worm resting on its bed of imported sand.
