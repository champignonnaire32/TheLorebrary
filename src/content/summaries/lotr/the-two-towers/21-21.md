### 21: Chapter X: The Choices of Master Samwise
Samwise Gamgee attacks the giant spider Shelob with Frodo's sword, Sting, as she attempts to drag his master's body away. He stabs at the spider's eyes and then slashes at her soft underbelly after she rears up in pain. As Shelob tries to crush Sam with her massive bulk, he holds Sting upward, causing the monster to impale herself on the elven-blade. Sam then uses the Phial of Galadriel, a crystal vessel filled with the light of Eärendil's star, to drive the blinded and wounded creature back into her lair. Finding Frodo cold and unresponsive, Sam discovers that Shelob has stung his master in the neck and concludes that Frodo is dead.

Sam removes the One Ring from Frodo's neck and resolves to complete the quest alone to prevent the Enemy from capturing the artifact. He places Frodo's sword and Faramir's staff beside the body before hiding the Phial of Galadriel and heading toward the mountain pass. As he reaches the Cleft of Cirith Ungol, a narrow passage between high stone walls, he hears two groups of Orcs approaching from different directions. Sam puts on the Ring to become invisible, which alters his perception by dimming his sight and heightening his hearing. He overhears a conversation between the orc-captains Shagrat and Gorbag, who lead companies from the tower of Cirith Ungol and Minas Morgul.

The Orcs discover Frodo's body and carry him toward the Undergate, a hidden entrance leading back to their watchtower. Sam follows them and overhears Shagrat explain that Shelob does not eat dead meat but instead paralyzes her victims with a specific venom. Realizing that Frodo is actually alive and merely in a deep swoon, Sam cries out in fury and runs back toward the Orcs with his sword drawn. He attempts to stop the Orcs before they enter the tower, but they pass through large brazen doors that slam shut and are barred from within. Sam collapses senseless against the gates of the tower, having failed to rescue his master from the hands of the Enemy.
