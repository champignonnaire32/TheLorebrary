# Chapter60-64_Summary

### Chapter 60: Dragon’s Maw
Darrow and Mustang are transported by military craft through a lunar storm to the Sovereign's spire on Luna. Cassius drags Darrow and the body of Sevro into the Dragon Maw, a deep underground bunker protected by the Zero Legion. Octavia au Lune stands in a command center surrounded by holographic generals while directing the defense against Darrow's fleet. Lysander, now ten years old, watches as the Jackal joins the group and the Ash Lord reports on the space battle. The Sovereign denounces Antonia for abandoning Roque at the Battle of the Core and sentences her to death.

Aja, the Sovereign’s Fury and primary bodyguard, strangles Antonia to death and tosses her body onto Sevro’s corpse. Mustang lies to the Sovereign, claiming ten thousand Obsidians have already landed via grain ships to assault the Citadel. Octavia orders her Praetorian guards to reinforce the surface, leaving only a few defenders in the bunker. The sanctum seals shut with a second set of thick, rotating metal doors to isolate the group. Cassius is sent to a side room to clean the blood off his clothing before a meal is served.

Darrow watches the holographic battle where the Ash Lord's fleet maintains a fifty-kilometer distance from the rebel ships. Octavia expresses nostalgia toward Cassius while dismissively noting Darrow's missing hand and injuries. The Jackal sits nearby, having brought his own fleet to hammer the rebels against the Ash Lord's anvil. Aja expresses concern about the Obsidian leader Sefi and her blood oath to kill the Fury. The Sovereign remains in the room with three Olympic Knights, Aja, the Jackal, Lysander, and the prisoners.

### Chapter 61: The Red
Aja drags Darrow to Octavia's feet and removes his muzzle for a live broadcast to the entire Society. The Jackal takes Sevro’s gun and prepares to execute Darrow while the Sovereign delivers a speech about order and hierarchy. Darrow howls as a signal, and Cassius suddenly kills three Praetorian guards with his hidden razor. Cassius uses a remote to release Darrow and Mustang from their metal restraint vests. Darrow lunges at Octavia and stabs her repeatedly in the stomach with a knife hidden in his vest.

Mustang shoots Aja with a pulseRifle, throwing the Fury across the room and into a conference table. Cassius kills the Truth Knight by skewering him through the eye socket while wearing only prison greens. Darrow tackles the Jackal and pins his left arm to the floor with his own razor. The Joy Knight wounds Cassius’s leg, but Darrow kills the knight by cleaving his body in half from behind. Aja rises from the wreckage of the table and prepares to defend the dying Sovereign.

Mustang, Cassius, and Darrow form a triangle to engage Aja, who uses the Willow Way sword style. Aja severely wounds all three, stabbing Darrow in the ribs and slashing Mustang’s stomach and knee. Cassius tosses Darrow a syringe containing a stimulant called snakebite to revive Sevro's faked death. Darrow crawls to Sevro’s body, cuts open his shirt, and plunges the needle into his chest. Sevro’s eyes burst open, and he immediately begins shouting about Darrow's missing hand and bleeding wounds.

**Sentence Count Check:** 15

### Chapter 62: Omnis Vir Lupus
Sevro explodes into motion and retrieves a pulseFist and his razor while charging Aja with a battle cry. He tramples the Jackal and joins Mustang, Cassius, and Darrow in a four-way assault against the Fury. The group methodically bleeds Aja out, targeting her tendons and limbs as she tries to protect the Sovereign. Sevro cuts Aja's Achilles tendon, Cassius slashes her knee, and Mustang severs her arm at the elbow. Sevro finishes the fight by driving two razors into Aja's chest and decapitating her.

Lysander tries to protect Octavia with a razor, but he eventually drops the weapon at Cassius's request. Cassius reveals to the dying Sovereign a holocube showing the Jackal's forces murdering the Bellona children. Darrow shows Octavia a recording of the destruction of Rhea to prove her own history of atrocities. Octavia warns Darrow that someone must rule to prevent chaos before she finally dies from her wounds. Darrow closes the Sovereign's eyes while the Jackal begins to laugh hysterically from his position on the floor.

The Jackal reveals he has hidden four hundred nuclear warheads across the moon Luna. He explains that Lilath is on the Lion of Mars and will detonate bombs if he is harmed. To prove his threat, the Jackal orders the detonation of a bomb in Mare Serenitatis, killing five million people. He then orders a second blast in Mare Anguis, causing another massive red blossom on the holographic display. The Jackal demands that Darrow kill himself in exchange for the lives of the remaining three billion lunar citizens.

### Chapter 63: Silence
Darrow refuses the Jackal's demand and instead tackles him to the floor to silence his commands. He jams his hand into the Jackal's mouth and rips out the man's tongue with his Helldiver grip. Darrow contacts Victra and orders her to destroy the Lion of Mars despite the risk to the fleet. Lilath continues to detonate nuclear bombs on the lunar surface, causing widespread fire and ash. Lysander suggests calling the Ash Lord to stop the destruction since the Sovereign is dead.

Mustang opens a communication channel to the Gold Praetors and the Ash Lord's command ship. She informs the fleet that the nuclear blasts are being caused by the Jackal's stolen arsenal. Lysander appears on the broadcast holding the Dawn Scepter, which is stained with Octavia's blood. He tells the Ash Lord that Mustang is no longer a traitor but their new conqueror. The Ash Lord and the other Praetors witness the evidence of the Jackal's betrayal on the live feed.

The combined rebel and loyalist fleets turn their weapons on the Lion of Mars and destroy the ship. Twelve more bombs detonate before Lilath's ship is finally silenced, leaving the moon in total turmoil. The Society begins to fracture as wealthy Praetors flee toward Venus, Mercury, and Mars in their personal vessels. Mustang stands under the holo of the wounded moon, realizing she must now take control of the chaos. Darrow comforts her, promising they will rebuild the Society together from the ruins.

### Chapter 64: Hail
Cassius opens the bunker doors using his Olympic code, and Sevro admits he soiled himself during the fake execution. Darrow carries the unconscious, tongueless Jackal on his shoulder as the group exits the inner sanctum. They encounter a hall of Praetorian guards who lower their weapons and part for Mustang. Mustang carries Octavia's severed head and the Dawn Scepter as she marches through the Citadel. Hundreds of soldiers and servants flock to her side as the only remaining symbol of authority.

The group enters the Senate Chamber, where the Senators are attempting to hold an emergency election. Mustang takes the podium, throws Octavia's head down, and declares a new age for the Society. Darrow, Cassius, Sevro, and Lysander all kneel before her to demonstrate her legitimacy as the new Sovereign. The Senate follows their lead, breaking into a collective shout of "Hail, Sovereign" to end the conflict. Mustang's ascension is finalized as the old hierarchy is dismantled and the Rising gains control.

One week later, Darrow and Mustang attend the public execution of the Jackal in a lunar square. Adrius is led to a metal scaffold where a White hangwoman prepares a noose for his neck. The crowd jeers and throws stones, but the Jackal remains silent as he stands on the trapdoor. Since the low gravity of Luna cannot break a neck, Mustang steps forward to pull her brother's feet. She whispers a final message to her twin before pulling down to end his life.
